# Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1 cup + 2 TBSP all-purpose flour
- 4 tablespoons Nutella 
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
